Happzee is an app-based survey tool designed to ease the cumbersome process of surveying a large pool of workers in an industry. Happzee came into existence after realising the need for making feedback collection a quick and effcient process. Our one-click app comes as a solution for both employers and the employees, where the former gets to check the pulse of their company and the latter can voice their concerns - all at their fingertips.
